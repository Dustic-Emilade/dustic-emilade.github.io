Power Washing

Practical power washing services for residential and small urban spaces, focused on refresh, safety, and appearance.

What This Service Includes

  • Porches and patios
  • Walkways and pathways
  • Driveways and sidewalks
  • Railings and exterior steps
  • Waterproof decks
  • Vehicle washing

Vehicle Washing

Vehicle washing may be included as part of a power washing service at no additional cost.

This may include a full exterior car wash when requested and when performed alongside another power washing task.

What This Service Does Not Include

  • Commercial or industrial pressure washing
  • Roofs or delicate historic surfaces
  • Non-waterproof decking or untreated wood
  • Graffiti removal or paint stripping
  • Chemical treatments requiring specialized licensing

Typical Pricing

Power washing services are priced per job, starting at $100.

Final pricing is based on surface area, material type, buildup level, and access.

Free quotes are always provided.

Bundle Options

Power washing may be bundled with yard and garden cleanup or other outdoor services.

Bundled services may qualify for discounted pricing.

See Bundles & Discounts for details.

Access & Safety

  • Water access must be available on-site
  • Work areas must be reasonably accessible
  • Children under 12 and pets must be secured away from work areas
  • Delicate items should be cleared prior to service
  • Services may be rescheduled if conditions are unsafe

All scope and pricing details are confirmed before work begins.
