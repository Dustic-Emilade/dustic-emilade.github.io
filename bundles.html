Important Pricing Information

All services are priced per job, not per hour.

Time estimates are provided for scheduling purposes only. Pricing is based on the scope of work discussed and does not change based on time spent.

We do not charge additional fees if a job takes longer than expected, as long as the scope matches what was originally discussed.

Additional charges may apply only if hidden or undisclosed work is discovered (for example, buried debris or rock piles that were not disclosed during the quote).

Any changes to scope or pricing will always be discussed before additional work is performed.
