Pet Sitting

Reliable, attentive care for pets while you’re away, focused on safety, routine, and clear communication.

Animals We Care For

  • Dogs
  • Cats
  • Indoor birds
  • Outdoor birds (including chickens, ducks, and turkeys)
  • Rabbits

Services Offered

  • Feeding and watering
  • Litter box and enclosure cleaning
  • Dog walking
  • Stay-at-home pet sitting (daytime visits only)
  • Shopping for pet food, supplies, and toys
  • Transportation to and from veterinary or grooming appointments

Important Limitations

  • Overnight pet sitting is not offered
  • Care is provided based on instructions given in advance
  • Animals must be reasonably manageable for safe handling

Health, Allergies & Restrictions

Clients must inform us of any known allergies, sensitivities, medical conditions, or behavioral restrictions prior to service.

This includes food restrictions, medication needs, and handling limitations.

Animal Welfare Notice

We are obligated to report visible signs of animal abuse or cruelty to appropriate emergency services.

This policy exists to protect animal welfare and ensure safe working conditions.

Typical Pricing

Pet sitting services are priced per visit, starting at $30 per visit.

Pricing may vary based on number of animals, travel distance, and services requested.

Free quotes are provided for multi-visit or extended care needs.

Safety & Access

  • Clear instructions must be provided prior to service
  • Pets must be secured if needed for safe entry and care
  • Unsafe or undisclosed conditions may result in service refusal

All care expectations and pricing are confirmed before service begins.
