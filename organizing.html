Cleaning & Organizing Help

Supportive, hands-on help for people who want to make their living spaces more functional, manageable, and livable.

How This Service Works

Cleaning and organizing help is a collaborative process.

This service is not about someone coming in and taking over. It works best when the client and worker actively work together to sort, clean, and reset spaces.

Progress depends on participation from both sides, with the goal of creating systems that are easier to maintain moving forward.

Cleaning Support Includes

  • Laundry assistance
  • Dishes and kitchen cleanup
  • Bathroom cleaning
  • Cabinet and drawer organization
  • Cleaning of already established and accessible areas

Organizing Support Includes

  • Room-by-room organization
  • Establishing what qualifies as garbage first
  • Sorting items into keep vs. remove categories
  • Creating simple, functional organization systems
  • Support for reducing clutter and accumulated items

Hoarding & Overwhelmed Spaces

This service can assist with the early stages of hoarding or overwhelming clutter by helping establish safe, manageable cleanup practices.

This is a gradual, respectful process and is approached without judgment.

Severe hoarding situations, biohazards, or unsafe environments are not included under this service.

What This Service Does Not Include

  • Full maid or housekeeping services
  • Biohazard or extreme hoarding cleanup
  • Disposal of items without client consent
  • Forced removal of personal belongings

Typical Pricing

Cleaning and organizing help is priced per job, starting at $100.

Pricing is based on the size of the space, level of clutter, and scope of work agreed upon.

Free quotes are always provided.

Important Notes

  • This service works best as a shared effort
  • Pacing and breaks are discussed as needed
  • All decisions regarding belongings remain with the client
  • Scope and goals are confirmed before work begins

Respect, communication, and collaboration are central to this service.
