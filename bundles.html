Bundles & Discounts

Bundled services are offered to make multi-service jobs more affordable and to reduce repeat setup and travel time.

All bundles are optional and discussed during your free quote.

Outdoor Service Bundles

Low Branch & Underbrush Trimming + Yard Cleanup

When low branch and underbrush trimming is booked together with yard or garden cleanup, the underbrush trimming portion receives a 30% discount.

Yard Cleanup + Power Washing

Booking yard cleanup and power washing during the same visit qualifies for a 15% discount on the combined total.

Indoor Service Bundles

Deep Cleaning + Refrigerator Cleanout

When refrigerator cleanout is added to a deep cleaning service, the refrigerator cleanout receives a 20% discount.

Multi-Room Cleaning & Organizing

Projects involving multiple rooms or areas may qualify for bundled pricing. Discounts vary based on layout and scope.

Mixed Indoor & Outdoor Bundles

Property Reset Bundle

A combined package for indoor and outdoor work completed during the same visit.

Property Reset Bundles may receive a 10–20% discount, depending on services included.

Important Pricing Information

All services are priced per job, not per hour.

Time estimates are provided to help with scheduling only. Final pricing is based on the scope of work discussed during the quote process and does not change based on time spent.

We do not charge additional fees if a job takes longer than expected, provided the scope of work matches what was originally discussed.

Additional charges may only apply if hidden or undisclosed work is discovered during service (for example, buried debris, hidden rock piles, or previously inaccessible areas not included in the quote).

Any changes to scope or pricing will always be discussed before additional work is performed.

Additional Notes

  • All discounts apply only when services are completed during the same visit
  • Discounts apply to bundled services only
  • Free quotes are always provided
  • Bundle availability may vary based on access and safety conditions
