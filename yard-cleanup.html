Yard & Garden Cleanup

Practical, hands-on help for maintaining, resetting, and caring for residential yards and gardens.

What This Service Includes

  • General yard cleanup and raking
  • Weeding and garden bed preparation
  • Planting and harvesting for home gardens
  • Pathway and walkway maintenance
  • Basic lawn care and upkeep
  • Fallen limb removal (up to 3-inch circumference)
  • Firewood moving or removal
  • Stone removal (up to 50 lbs per stone)
  • Coop maintenance and cleanup
  • Fence repair and construction (see note below)
  • Patio furniture setup, moving, and cleanup

What Qualifies as Patio Furniture

  • Tables and chairs
  • Patio sofas and seating sets
  • Collapsible gazebos
  • Porch and patio umbrellas
  • Outdoor decorative items

Fence Work Clarification

Fence repair and construction services are available, including full fence builds if needed.

We are not licensed contractors, and for large or complex fence projects, consulting a professional contractor is recommended.

Fence work performed under this service is discussed in detail during the quote process to ensure expectations are clear.

What This Service Does Not Include

  • Orchard maintenance or agricultural operations
  • Pond cleaning, maintenance, or dredging
  • Heavy landscaping or grading
  • Tree removal or large limb cutting
  • Commercial or industrial property work

Typical Pricing

Yard and garden cleanup services are priced per job, starting at $150.

Final pricing is based on the size of the area, scope of work, access conditions, and disposal needs.

Free quotes are always provided.

Bundle Options

Yard and garden cleanup may be bundled with low branch and underbrush trimming or power washing services.

Bundled services may qualify for discounted pricing.

See Bundles & Discounts for details.

Access & Safety

  • Work areas must be reasonably accessible
  • Children under 12 and pets must be secured away from work areas
  • Undisclosed or hidden work will be discussed before proceeding
  • Services may be rescheduled if conditions are unsafe

All scope and pricing details are confirmed before work begins.
