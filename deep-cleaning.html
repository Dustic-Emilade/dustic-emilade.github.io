Deep Cleaning

Thorough, detail-focused cleaning intended to reset and refresh living spaces beyond routine maintenance.

What This Service Includes

  • Toilets, showers, tubs, and sinks
  • Refrigerators (interior and exterior)
  • Appliances and appliance surfaces
  • Tile and grout
  • Walls and baseboards
  • Cabinets and drawers (interior and exterior)
  • Hardwood and other flooring surfaces
  • Vents and light fixtures
  • Windows (interior and exterior)
  • Behind and beneath furniture when accessible
  • Sticky residue, stains, and pet hair

Windows & Ventilation Requirements

Proper ventilation is required during deep cleaning due to the use of cleaning agents.

Windows must be able to open in any room requested for deep cleaning. For small or enclosed rooms, a door must be present.

Larger spaces should have a nearby door or a large window to allow adequate airflow.

Flooring & Surface Care

Clients must inform us in advance of any fragile or specialty finishes, particularly for hardwood or sealed surfaces.

Cleaning methods may be adjusted based on surface type to prevent damage.

Pets & Children

  • Pets must be secured away from areas where cleaning agents are used
  • It is strongly recommended that children are kept out of active cleaning areas
  • Work may be paused if safety conditions cannot be maintained

What This Service Does Not Include

  • Biohazard or hoarding cleanup
  • Mold remediation
  • Carpet replacement or repair
  • Structural repairs

Typical Pricing

Deep cleaning services are priced per job, starting at $150.

Final pricing is based on room size, condition, surface types, and access.

Free quotes are always provided.

Bundle Options

Deep cleaning may be bundled with refrigerator cleanout or other indoor services.

Bundled services may qualify for discounted pricing.
