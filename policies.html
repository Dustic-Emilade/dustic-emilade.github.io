Service Policies & Safety Guidelines

These guidelines exist to ensure a safe, respectful, and professional working environment for both clients and service providers.

Our Values & Approach

Grays Country Services for Blue Country is operated by young individuals committed to honest work, personal responsibility, and mutual respect.

Our work is guided by Christian values of service, integrity, and care for others. We strive to be welcoming and inclusive to all clients, regardless of background or personal beliefs.

Inclusivity & Non-Discrimination

  • We do not deny services based on gender, religion, political views, or lifestyle choices
  • All clients are treated with respect and professionalism
  • All workers are entitled to the same respect in return

Safety First

  • The safety of workers, clients, and animals is our top priority
  • Work may be declined or discontinued if unsafe conditions are present
  • Clients are expected to provide honest and accurate information about the work environment

Substances & Conduct

Our workers do not smoke, drink, or use recreational substances while on the job.

Services may be refused or discontinued if there are safety concerns, including suspected substance use, impaired behavior, or unsafe conduct at the service location.

Boundaries & Respect

  • Any form of harassment, misconduct, or inappropriate behavior will result in immediate service refusal
  • Pressuring workers beyond agreed scope, pricing, or comfort levels is not acceptable
  • Attempts to take advantage of workers will result in service termination

Right to Refuse Service

We reserve the right to decline or stop services at any time if:

  • Safety concerns arise
  • Misconduct or inappropriate behavior occurs
  • Workers feel unsafe, pressured, or disrespected
  • The work environment differs significantly from what was described

This policy exists to protect everyone involved.

Scope & Communication

  • Service scope and expectations are discussed before work begins
  • Changes to scope or conditions must be communicated clearly
  • All pricing and service decisions are based on the agreed scope

By requesting services, clients acknowledge these guidelines and agree to engage respectfully and honestly.
