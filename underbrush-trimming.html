Low Branch & Underbrush Trimming

Clearing low branches and manageable undergrowth to improve access, safety, and usability of outdoor spaces.

What Counts as Underbrush

  • Light to moderate berry growth
  • Low shrubs and bushes
  • Saplings and young tree growth
  • Brambles and tangled ground vegetation
  • Undergrowth up to 2 feet tall

What This Service Includes

  • Trimming and removal of low branches
  • Clearing brush and manageable undergrowth
  • Piling debris on-site or removal discussed during quote
  • Basic cleanup of the immediate work area

What This Service Does Not Include

  • Mature tree removal
  • Overhead or high-limb trimming
  • Climbing or work at height
  • Stump grinding or root removal
  • Heavy machinery use
  • Dense or heavy berry thickets requiring specialized equipment

Poisonous Plant Policy

Clearing of poisonous or skin-irritating plants is not offered.

This includes, but is not limited to, poison oak, poison ivy, and poison sumac.

These plants require specialized protective equipment and professional handling to safely remove.

Typical Pricing

Services are priced per job, starting at $100.

Final pricing is based on the size of the area, density of growth, access conditions, and disposal needs.

Free quotes are always provided.

Bundle Options

Low branch and underbrush trimming may be bundled with yard & garden cleanup services.

When bundled, the underbrush trimming portion receives a 30% discount.

See Bundles & Discounts for details.

Access & Safety

  • Work is limited to growth reachable from the ground
  • Work areas must be reasonably accessible
  • Children under 12 and pets must be secured away from work areas
  • Services may be rescheduled if conditions are unsafe

All scope and pricing details are confirmed before work begins.
