Low Branch & Underbrush Trimming

Clearing low branches, brush, and ground-level overgrowth to improve access, safety, and usability of outdoor spaces.

Typical Pricing

Services are priced per job, starting at $100.

Final quotes are based on square footage, density of growth, ease of access, and disposal needs.

Free quotes are always provided.

What This Service Includes

  • Trimming and removal of low branches
  • Clearing brush and undergrowth
  • Pile consolidation or removal (as discussed during quote)
  • Basic cleanup of the work area

Service Scope & Limitations

  • Work is limited to low branches and ground-level growth
  • No tall tree trimming or overhead limb removal
  • No work requiring climbing or heavy machinery
  • Work areas must be reasonably accessible

Bundle Options

Low branch and underbrush trimming may be bundled with yard & garden cleanup services.

When bundled, underbrush trimming receives a 30% discount.

Safety & Access

  • Children under 12 and pets must be secured away from work areas
  • Heavy underbrush must be accessible for safe trimming
  • Services may be rescheduled if conditions are unsafe

All pricing and scope details are confirmed before work begins.
