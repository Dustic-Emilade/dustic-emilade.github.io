Grays Country Services for Blue Country

Honest, hands-on help for homes, land, and everyday upkeep. Serving folks who need an extra set of capable hands.

Serving: Amador, Calaveras, Alpine, Sacramento, Placer, Yolo, and Solano counties

Outdoor Services

  • Tree trimming (light to moderate)
  • Yard and garden cleanup
  • Gutter and roof cleanup
  • Berry bush removal
  • Rock removal
  • Wire fencing setup and repair
  • Power washing (porches & patios)
  • Outdoor furniture setup
  • Car washing
  • Firewood moving or removal
  • Chicken coop cleanup
  • Equine sitting and basic care

Indoor Services

  • Cleaning and organizing assistance
  • Deep cleaning
  • Refrigerator cleanouts
  • Pet sitting for birds, cats, dogs, rabbits, and reptiles

Please note: Rodent care is not offered.

Safety Notice

For everyone’s safety, please ensure children under 12 and pets are secured in a safe area while tools or equipment are being used.

If you need steady, respectful help without the runaround, you’re in the right place.
